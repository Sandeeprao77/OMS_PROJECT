Welcome to VITA OMS

Who We Are?

"VITA" can refer to several different technologies and organizations. Primarily, it refers to VITA, an organization that develops and promotes open standards for real-time, a telepresence robot used in healthcare. There's also Vita Technologies, a company offering IT solutions, and VITA Technologies Limited, a manufacturing company in Bangalore. Finally, VITA is also the name of the Virginia Information Technologies Agency.

What We Do?

Developing and promoting open standards for real-time, modular, embedded computing systems.

Developing and promoting open technology standards, improving interoperability, and advancing common technology.

Focus: Providing IT solutions, including web development, mobile app development, and cybersecurity network solutions.
